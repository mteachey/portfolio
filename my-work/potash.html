Potash America

Description

Process

This Wordpress site was developed by customizing the Divi theme in order to provide the client with a unique site that also met their budget.

The nav menu, video banner with cut out, home page stats section, and the layout of the Key Sectors page were some of the theme's features that had to be customized in order to match the design.

There is one call to their stock API that was coded to enable their current stock price to be dynamically listed at the top of the site. This has since been removed and will be added back in a later version.

Future Versions

Future versions of the site will enable users to purchase stock from the site.
