About

I am one of those truly well-rounded, good-at-it all individuals. I am an experienced front-end developer who loves getting knee-deep in code and coming up hours later. I have a knack for designing eye-loving layouts with an intuitive understanding of good user experience. On top of all of that, I am good at connecting and communicating with people.

I have been working as a developer, honing my skills for four years now. For two of those years, I was a website manager and developer for a high-paced marketing firm. I was their lead developer on several site refreshes and new sites and managed over 50 websites on various CMSs and platforms. I coordinated multiple projects with a wide range of tasks, constraints, and budgets. I was the key decision-maker on platform selection, hosting, security, and UX design for our clients and consulted directly with our clients on the best web-related products for their business and budgets.

Currently, I am self-employed creating client-pleasing websites, applications, and experiences for clients while I add to my skill set to become a full-stack developer. I recently built a web app - weekend roulette - to help take the decision making out of your weekend. It uses several free APIs to help you plan your weekend.

Before pursuing my love of code, I was a middle school science teacher and chemical engineering graduate from Virginia Tech. I have had the fortune to live and work in such amazing cities as New York and Washington, D.C., as well as, small, mountain towns like Boone, NC. I currently call Denver, CO my home and love it! When I am not working with code, you can usually find me enjoying the outdoors with my dog and a pair of hiking shoes or a bike.
